About Me

The story behind Quilts by Gina

Hi, I’m Gina! I’ve been quilting for many years, starting with small projects and gradually moving on to larger, more detailed quilts. What started as a hobby quickly grew into a passion, and now I create custom quilts for families, friends, and clients who want something truly meaningful and unique.

I specialize in T-shirt quilts, baby quilts, wall hangings, and more. Each quilt is made with love and attention to detail, using high-quality fabrics and professional quilting techniques. My goal is to create something that tells your story and lasts for generations.

When I’m not quilting, I enjoy spending time with my family, exploring new patterns, and teaching others about the joy of quilting. Whether you’re looking for a memory quilt to preserve cherished shirts or a special handmade gift, I’d love to help bring your vision to life.
